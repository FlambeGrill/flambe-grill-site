About Flambé Grill

Flambé Grill was born from a passion for real fire, charcoal and the feeling you get when food is cooked in front of you. We started catering events in Montreal and now bring the same energy to South Florida.

We handle the fire, timing and cooking. You enjoy the show, the aromas and plates that go straight from the grill to your guests.

  • Live fire catering for private and corporate events
  • High quality meat and fresh ingredients
  • We bring equipment, grills and spits to you
  • Ideal for bachelor parties, golf events and special occasions
